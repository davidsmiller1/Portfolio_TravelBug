Background

Goal

Design a mobile application prototype to help users search and select travel options that have a Covid Safety Rating based on research of company best practices and public health information.

4 weeks

Launch & Research

In this research project, 9 interviews and surveys were conducted. As a result of the research it was discovered that:

  • Most people want to travel, but won’t if they don’t feel safe
  • To feel safe most people want assurances that the transportation and hospitality industries are compiling with public health best practices
  • This is especially true for families with children who want assurances even after the pandemic ends

Problem Statement

Therefore, we believe providing consistent and readily available information about the travel and hospitality industry’s compliance to public health best practices for families with children will achieve an increase in leisure travel during and after the global pandemic.

Definition & Ideation

Based on user research, it was determined that there are a lot of solutions to help make people feel safer to travel during and after the pandemic. Mostly this includes companies following clearly defined public health best practices. Through the brainstorming process, one idea became apparent as important: a trustworthy, independent travel app that saves time for people researching travel options during the pandemic and provides a Covid Safety Rating for travel and hospitality companies based on public health best practices.

Wireframe & Test

A user flow informed the creation of a wireframe. The process of signing up for an account and searching for travel options using a Covid Safety Rating filter was the primary design for a low fidelity prototype.

Guerrilla testing was conducted on 5 users. Based on the testing results, 90% of users completed Task 1 to successfully sign up for the app and sign in under 1 minute passing the 80% success rate. Additionally, 100% of users stated that the Covid Safety Rating was somewhat easy or very easy to find passing the 80% success rate for Task 2. Overall the app was received positively. Most functions worked properly. Users provided helpful feedback about minor design and function changes which is outlined in the rest of this report.

Prototype & Delivery

Based on user testing, several adjustments were made and integrated into the high fidelity prototype. These included:

  • Redesigning the welcome pages with more clear messaging and call to actions. Full screen images were added and bright colors were used to make the welcome pages happier.
  • The sign in/sign up process was streamlined into one clickable screen. The layout was redesigned with more clear messaging and call to actions.
  • The first appearance of the Covid Safety Rating was moved from the search results function to the main app page. It was also added as a filter option and given more prevalence on results pages.

Conclusion

Takeaways

Through the ideation and design process, it has become clear that there is a need for an app that serves this type of purpose: to inform travelers, especially families with children, about safe travel options, so they can travel again during or after the pandemic. Current travel apps do not have a feature like this.

Next Steps

In order for TravelBug to function properly, research must now be conducted on the public health best practices and contact tracing for each hospitality and travel company that would like to participate.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, despite the Covid-19 global pandemic, there is demand from families with children to be able to travel again and take the once prized family vacation. For many families, safety is their top concern, and the travel app, TravelBug can support them in making the best decisions to keep everyone healthy while planning vacations again. The app is well received in user testing. Many functional and aesthetic design changes were made from ideation to prototyping to refine the usability of the app. Once complete, TravelBug will help to bring happiness to families seeking to safely leave the confines of their homes, and to revive an economy that has been negatively impacted by the Covid-19 global pandemic.
